Fashion

Fashion is a term used to describe the creation of clothing, footwear, accessories, cosmetics, and jewelry of different cultural aesthetics and their mix and match into outfits that depict distinctive ways of dressing as signifiers of social status, self-expression, and group belonging¹.

Arts

Fashion and art have a long-standing relationship, with many fashion designers drawing inspiration from art movements to create their collections¹. Fashion is regarded as a part of visual culture, a kind of body-art that reflects changes in contemporary culture².
